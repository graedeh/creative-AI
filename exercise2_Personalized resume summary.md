## Objective: Apply prompting to professional use cases.

1. Write a prompt that generates a resume summary based on role and skills. Example: "Create a 3-sentence professional summary for a software engineer skilled in Python and cloud systems."

**prompt**
"creat a 3-sentence profrssional summary for a software engineer skilled in golang and rust"

Experienced in developing concurrent, memory-safe applications, APIs, and distributed services with a focus on efficiency and clean architecture. Proven ability to collaborate across teams to deliver robust backend solutions that meet demanding technical and business requirements.

2. Adjust the prompt for a different role (e.g., teacher, designer, marketer).

**OUTPUT**
A dedicated and passionate educator with expertise in classroom management and creating engaging, student-centered curriculum. Skilled in fostering a positive learning environment while adapting instructional strategies to meet diverse student needs. Committed to continuous professional development and promoting academic success through innovative teaching practices.

3. Compare how the outputs change depending on the role.

3. Tone: Technical, performance-driven, and collaborative, showcasing problem-solving and engineering skills.

1. Focus: Highlights teaching strategies, classroom management, and curriculum development.
