## Objective: Create prompts for models that generate images.

1. Write a descriptive prompt for an image model (e.g., "A futuristic city skyline at sunset in watercolor style.").


## Vague Prompt:
    "A futuristic city skyline at sunset."

## Refined Prompt:

    "A futuristic city skyline at sunset, painted in watercolor style, with glowing neon lights, tall skyscrapers, and a bird's-eye view perspective."

## Comparing the OutPuts:
    Vague prompts result in more general, flexible, or ambiguous images that may not fully meet your expectations while Refined prompts lead to more detailed, customized outputs that closely align with the visual style and characteristics you want.
